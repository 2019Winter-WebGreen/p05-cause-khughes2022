Fostering Dogs

Why Should I Foster A Dog?

Dogs multiply daily. The average litter size is 5 puppies. There are not nearly enough shelters for these dogs. Fostering dogs can save their lives and give them a nice, loving home and family until they can receive a permanent one. Not only can fostering save the dogs' lives, but a dog is a man's best friend! Dogs are known to comfort those with anxiety, serve as a guide and protection for the blind, relieve stress, and aid those with mental disabilities or disorders. By fostering, you can save the pooch and also yourself.




Where Do I Foster From?

The Purpose Of A Dog

No shelter has enough room for every stray that walks in. And if they do, some shelters abuse the dogs. By fostering, you can save a dogs life so that it can save others'.
